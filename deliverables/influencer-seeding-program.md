# Forever Cables Influencer Product Seeding Program

A complete process for sending free cables to creators and reviewers in exchange for honest coverage. No payment, no quid pro quo required.

---

## 1. TIER CRITERIA: WHO QUALIFIES

### Tier A: Priority Seeds (send proactively)

- **Platform:** YouTube, Instagram, TikTok
- **Followers/subscribers:** 10,000 to 100,000
- **Content fit:** Posts pedalboard builds, gear demos, rig rundowns, or cable/signal chain content
- **Engagement:** Comment-to-view ratio above 0.5%, or consistent community interaction
- **Prior coverage:** Has reviewed boutique or hand-built gear before
- **Bonus signals:** Mentions cable quality, tone transparency, or lifetime warranties in existing content

### Tier B: Selective Seeds (send on request or after warm-up)

- **Followers/subscribers:** 100,000 to 500,000
- **Content fit:** Broader guitar or bass content; gear is one of several topics
- **Engagement:** Active community, responds to comments
- **Approach:** Warm up through community engagement (comments, shares) before offering product

### Tier C: Aspirational Seeds (long game)

- **Followers/subscribers:** 500,000+
- **Content fit:** Major guitar/gear channels (That Pedal Show, JHS, Rhett Shull tier)
- **Approach:** Do not cold-pitch. Build brand awareness through Tier A and B coverage first. When multiple smaller creators have featured Forever Cables, reach out with social proof.

### Disqualifiers (any tier)

- Channel has no gear content in the last 6 months
- Creator primarily does paid sponsorships with no organic reviews
- Audience is not guitar, bass, or pedalboard focused
- Creator has a history of only negative/clickbait reviews

## 2. WHAT TO SEND

### Standard Seed Kit

Send one of the following based on the creator's content:

| Creator Type | Send This | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Pedalboard builder | 1x Workhorse (6") + 1x Right Angle (6") | Shows both connector styles on a real board |
| Gigging/touring musician | 1x Stage Cable (10ft) | Most visible on stage; easiest to demo |
| Studio/recording creator | 1x Stage Cable (15ft) + 1x Workhorse (12") | Covers instrument and pedalboard use |
| General gear reviewer | 1x Pedalboard Pack | Full product line in one box; best unboxing |

### What goes in the box

1. The cable(s)
2. Product insert card (HAT-96 postcard variant)
3. A short handwritten note (2 sentences max; personalize with a reference to their content)
4. No press release. No sell sheet. No business card stack.

### Estimated cost per seed

- Single cable seed: $18 to $49 product cost + ~$8 shipping = $26 to $57 total
- Pedalboard Pack seed: $95 product cost + ~$10 shipping = $105 total
- Budget 20 seeds for launch: approximately $600 to $1,200 total

## 3. OUTREACH SEQUENCE

Building on the HAT-62 YouTube outreach templates. This sequence covers all platforms.

### Step 1: Warm-up (Days 1 to 7)

Before sending any message:
- Follow the creator on their primary platform
- Leave 2 to 3 genuine comments on recent posts (about their content, not your product)
- Share one of their posts or videos if relevant

### Step 2: Initial outreach email (Day 7 to 10)

**Subject line options:**
- "Hand-built cables for your board, on us"
- "Forever Cables; would love to send you one"
- "Quick question about your pedalboard setup"

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

I have been watching your [specific content reference, e.g., "pedalboard build series"] and really dig what you are doing.

I build guitar cables by hand here in the US. The line is called Forever Cables. Every cable uses Canare GS-6 or Mogami W2524 wire with Neutrik connectors, and every one comes with a lifetime guarantee. If it ever fails, we replace it, no questions asked.

I would love to send you a [specific product] to try on your board. No strings attached; if you like it, great. If not, keep it anyway.

If you are interested, just reply with your shipping address and I will get it out this week.

[Your name]
Forever Cables by Hatch Patch Cables
liferline.com

### Step 3: Follow-up (Day 14 to 17, if no reply)

**Subject:** "Re: [original subject]"

**Body:**

Hi [Name],

Just bumping this in case it got buried. Happy to send over a Forever Cable for you to try. No review required, no strings. Just want to get it in your hands.

Let me know if you are interested.

[Your name]

### Step 4: Final follow-up (Day 28, if no reply)

Do not send a third email. Instead:
- Engage with one more piece of their content genuinely
- Move them to the "revisit in 90 days" list
- If they respond later, pick up immediately

## 4. TRACKING SHEET TEMPLATE

Use a simple spreadsheet (Google Sheets or Airtable). One row per creator.

| Column | What to Track |
|---|---|
| Creator Name | Full name or channel name |
| Platform | YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc. |
| Handle/URL | Direct link to profile |
| Tier | A, B, or C |
| Followers | Subscriber or follower count at time of outreach |
| Content Fit | Pedalboard, gigging, studio, general gear |
| Warm-up Started | Date of first genuine engagement |
| Outreach Sent | Date of initial email/DM |
| Follow-up Sent | Date of follow-up email/DM |
| Response | Yes / No / Pending |
| Product Sent | Which SKU(s) shipped |
| Ship Date | When the package went out |
| Tracking Number | USPS tracking |
| Coverage Date | When they posted about it (if ever) |
| Coverage Type | Video review, Instagram story, mention, etc. |
| Coverage Link | URL to the post or video |
| Notes | Anything relevant (e.g., "said they will include in next pedalboard video") |

### Status tags

- **Warm-up:** Engaging with their content before outreach
- **Outreach sent:** Initial email/DM delivered
- **Awaiting reply:** Follow-up sent, waiting
- **Shipping:** Address received, cable going out
- **Delivered:** Package confirmed delivered
- **Posted:** Creator published coverage
- **No response:** No reply after full sequence; on 90-day revisit list
- **Declined:** Creator said no thanks

## 5. FOLLOW-UP SOP: ASKING FOR A REVIEW WITHOUT BEING PUSHY

### After the cable ships

- Send a short message with the tracking number: "Your Forever Cable is on the way. Tracking: [number]. No rush on anything; just wanted you to know it shipped."

### One week after delivery

- Check if they have posted anything. If yes, like, comment, and share it. Send a quick thank-you DM.
- If no post yet, do nothing. Do not ask.

### Three weeks after delivery

- If still no coverage, send one light check-in:

"Hey [Name], hope the cable is treating you well. No pressure at all on a review. If you do end up mentioning it at some point, that would mean a lot to us as a small brand. Either way, enjoy it."

### After that

- Do not follow up again about coverage. The cable is a gift, not a transaction.
- Continue engaging with their content naturally.
- If they post about it months later, respond warmly and share it.

### If they post a review

1. Like and comment within 24 hours (genuine, not salesy)
2. Share to Forever Cables social accounts with credit
3. Send a thank-you DM: "Just saw your post. Really appreciate it. If you ever need another cable or want to try a different length, just let us know."
4. Update the tracking sheet with coverage date, type, and link
5. Move them to the "repeat seeding" list for future product launches

### Rules

- Never pay for coverage. Ever.
- Never require a review in exchange for product.
- Never script what they should say.
- Never ask them to remove negative feedback.
- If a creator gives an honest negative review, thank them for the feedback and use it to improve.
