## 3. Warranty Registration Prompt

Warranty registration is optional (the guarantee requires no receipt or registration), but collecting registration data helps with customer records and future outreach.

### In-Package Prompt (on warranty card back)

**Register your cable** (optional, not required for warranty coverage):
Visit liferline.com/guarantee or email support@liferline.com with your name and order number.

### Email Prompt (in Email 2: Shipping Notification, as a soft CTA)

Your guarantee is already active. No registration needed. But if you want to be on file for the fastest possible replacement, you can register at liferline.com/guarantee.

### Website Registration Flow (liferline.com/guarantee)

**Heading:** Register Your Forever Cable
**Subheading:** Not required for warranty coverage. Registration just means faster replacement if you ever need it.

**Fields:**
- Name
- Email
- Order number (optional)
- Product purchased (dropdown)
- Purchase date (optional)

**Submit button:** Register My Cable

**Confirmation message:** You are all set. Your cable is registered. If you ever need a replacement, email support@liferline.com and reference your registration. We will ship a new one before you send the old one back.

## 5. Personalization at Small Scale

At low volume (under 50 orders/month), personal touches are a competitive advantage over mass-market cable companies. These are low-cost, high-impact additions.

### Handwritten Thank-You Note

Write a short note on a blank card or the back of the packing slip. Keep it 1-2 sentences.

**Examples:**
- "Thanks for the order, [Name]. This one tested clean, enjoy it."
- "Hope this Workhorse treats you well. Let me know if you ever need anything."
- "First Pedalboard Pack order this week. Appreciate the trust."

**Rules:**
- Use first name if available
- Reference the specific product they ordered
- Keep it casual and genuine; do not use marketing language
- Blue or black ink on cream or white card stock

### Order-Specific Touches

| Order type | Personal touch |
|---|---|
| First-time buyer | Handwritten note + warranty card + insert card |
| Repeat buyer | Handwritten note mentioning their loyalty ("Welcome back") |
| Pedalboard Pack (bundle) | Include an extra cable tie or velcro strip as a freebie |
| Stage Cable | Include a cable label tag if available |
| Custom build | Handwritten note describing the build ("Mogami W2524, 15ft, gold Neutrik") |

### When to Stop Handwriting

Once order volume makes handwritten notes impractical (roughly 50+ orders/week), switch to a printed card with a handwritten first name and signature. The card should still feel personal.

**Printed card template:**

Thank you for choosing Forever Cables. This cable was hand-soldered, tested, and inspected before it shipped. It is guaranteed for life.

If it ever fails, email support@liferline.com. We will send a replacement before you ship the old one back.

[Handwritten: first name + signature]

## Implementation Notes

- **Email platform:** Use the platform set up in HAT-158. All emails should come from a reply-able address (support@liferline.com or hello@liferline.com).
- **Personalization tokens:** [First Name], [Product Name], [Order Number], [Tracking Link]. Fall back to "there" if first name is not available ("Hey there").
- **Unsubscribe:** Transactional emails (order confirmation, shipping) do not need unsubscribe links. Marketing emails (review request) must include one.
- **Tone:** Casual, direct, confident. No jargon. Write like a person, not a brand.
- **Never use em dashes, double dashes, or triple dashes in any email copy.** Use commas, semicolons, colons, or separate sentences instead.
